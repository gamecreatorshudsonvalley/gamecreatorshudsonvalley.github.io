FAQ

What is the One Page RPG Jam?

A game jam where designers create a complete tabletop RPG that fits on a single page. Learn more on the official jam site.

What's the prize?

This jam is unranked and unjudged. All participants who submit a game will recieve a complete 7-piece dice set from Mana Potion Tabletop. One prize per participant.

Do I need tabletop RPG design experience to join?

No — all skill levels are welcome, from first-timers to experienced designers.

Is there a cost to attend?

Everything is free!

Can I join remotely?

Yes, these are hybrid events. Each calendar listing includes a virtual join link alongside the in-person location.

Where will events be held?

In-person sessions are hosted at Mana Potion Tabletop. Exact addresses are listed on each event in the calendar above.

What should I bring?

Just yourself and any notes or ideas you're working on — paper and pens will be available on site.
